Our Ethos

As the third generation of coffee entrepreneurs, we have set out to un-complicate coffee, while staying rooted in the values of a small business. Our goal is to make good coffee accessible to all and home brewing an enjoyable part of one’s daily routine.

Kings Roast

Classic filter kaapi, reimagined. A blend of Robusta AA and Arabica AA that sets a new standard for quality and excellence!

Best brewed in an Indian Filter or a Moka Pot.

Legacy Blend

Premium washed Arabica and Robusta with a hint of Peaberry beans.

With a subtle chocolatey notes, this house blend can be enjoyed with milk or black.

Brindavan Bold

If you are a traditional Kaapi enthusiast, our most beloved roast is just for you.

Best brewed in an Indian Filter or a Moka Pot. Experienced best with hot frothy milk.
